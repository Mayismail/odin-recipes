Biryani Recipe

Description

Biryani is a popular dish all across the Indian ,which is a spiced mix of meat and rice, traditionally cooked over an open fire in a leather pot..
When it comes to describing taste of this savoury dish, it gets limited to few words like delicious, lip smacking, awesome, spicy, mouthwatering, moist, dry, succulent

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled basmati rice
  • 600 gm chicken
  • 1/2 teaspoon mint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala powder
  • salt as required
  • 1 teaspoon saffron
  • 2 tablespoon refined oil
  • 1 tablespoon bay leaf
  • 1 black cardamom
  • 2 clove
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 onion
  • 4 green chillies
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • water as required

Steps

  1. soak saffron in water to prepare saffron water.
  2. heat refined oil in a deep bottomed pan
  3. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, green cardamom, cloves in it, and saute for about a minute.
  4. Add chopped onion in it and saute until pink.
  5. add chicken into it with slit green chillies, turmeric, salt to taste, ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, and green chilli paste.
    Mix well all the spices and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add hung curd into it
  7. Turn the flame to medium again and add garam masala in it along with ginger julienned, coriander and mint leaves.
  8. Add saffron water in it. Cook till the chicken is tender.
  9. add 1 cup cooked rice and spread evenly.
  10. add saffron water and pour ghee over it.
  11. Cook for 15-20 minutes with closed lid and garnish with 1 tbsp fried onions and coriander leaves.
  12. Serve hot chicken biryani.
