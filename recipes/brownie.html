Brownie Recipe

Description

Worlds Best Fudgiest Brownies is my best brownie recipe

Ingredients

  • butter
  • suger
  • Cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and vanilla
  • All-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Melt the butter with sugar, salt, and cocoa powder.
  2. Add the vanilla and eggs.
  3. Add the flour.After mixing the flour into the batter, I beat the batter with a spoon for 40 to 50 strokes. The batter will be thick.
  4. bake in the oven for 20 to 25 minutes
